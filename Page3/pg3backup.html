OsloMet Cafeteria

Welcome to the OsloMet Cafeteria

About the cafeteria

The cafeteria is a place for students, faculty, and staff to enjoy delicious and nutritious meals. They offer a wide variety of menu options, from healthy salads to mouth-watering daily specials.

Menu

The menu includes:

  • Breakfast items
  • Snack items
  • Lunch and dinner options
  • Vegetarian and vegan choices
  • Desserts and beverages

Location

They have several conveniently located cafeterias placed around the university campus including Buildings P32, P35, P38, P52 and SG26.

Opening Hours

They are open on weekdays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. All OsloMet students get a small discount!
