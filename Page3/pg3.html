Libraries

There are 3 university libraries located on the Pilestredet park campus. The main library is located in P48, and the secondary library in P35 on the 3rd floor, and the health sciences library is in P32.

Cafeterias

There are 5 cafeterias conveniently located around the Pilestredet park campus. All OsloMet students get a small discount!
