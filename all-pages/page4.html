The Cafeteria in P35

The cafeteria in P35 offers a wide selection of beverages, snacks and food. It is a popular place for students to visit during the day. It is located on the -1 floor according to the elevator. As normal people would call it, the first floor. It can be accessed from three sides. Below is a photo of the main entrance.

(The main entrance to the cafeteria. Photo: Julia Kræmer)
